About Us

Welcome to planta your smart companion for happier, healthier plants! We know how easy it is to forget when each plant needs water — especially when every species has its own schedule. That’s why we created planta, a simple, intuitive app that helps you stay on top of your plant care routine, without the guesswork.



What We Do


🌿Personalized Watering Schedules

Based on the plant type, location, and season, we help you create a care routine that’s just right for each plant.


📅 Timely Reminders

Never miss a watering day again! Get custom alerts when it’s time to water, mist, or fertilize.


📸 Plant Profiles

Keep track of all your plants with photos, notes, and history — your own mini garden journal.


☁️ Cloud Sync & Backup

Switching devices? Don’t worry — your data stays safe and synced across all your devices.
